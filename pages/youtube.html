Follow these simple steps to upload your coding tutorial or project video to YouTube:

  • 2 Click the Create button (camera icon with +) at the top right
  • 3 Select Upload Video and choose your video file
  • 4 Add a title, description, tags and thumbnail for your video
  • 5 Set visibility to Public and click Publish!
