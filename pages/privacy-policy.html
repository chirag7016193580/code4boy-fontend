1. Introduction

Welcome to Code4Boy ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website code4boy-web.vercel.app (the "Site").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Google Sign-In Data: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Contact Form: If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email, and message content.
  • User-Added Tutorials: Any tutorials you add through your profile are stored locally.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser Local Storage: We store your preferences (theme, favorites, watched tutorials, profile data) in your browser's local storage.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for theme preferences and basic analytics.
  • Usage Data: General usage patterns such as pages visited and time spent.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our educational platform
  • To personalize your learning experience (favorites, watched tutorials, progress tracking)
  • To authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In
  • To respond to your inquiries and contact form messages
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To detect and prevent security issues

4. Data Storage

Most of your data (profile, favorites, watched tutorials, preferences) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We do not transfer your personal data to external servers
  • Clearing your browser data will remove your stored preferences
  • Data is not synced across different browsers or devices

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Identity Services: For user authentication via Google Sign-In. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Google.
  • YouTube: Embedded videos are served by YouTube. YouTube's privacy policy applies.
  • Vercel: Our website is hosted on Vercel's platform.
  • CDN Services: We use Cloudflare and Google Fonts CDNs for delivering CSS and font resources.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • Domain restriction for authentication
  • Rate limiting on sign-in attempts
  • Input sanitization to prevent XSS attacks
  • HTTPS encryption for all connections

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences interested in learning coding. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in localStorage (visible in browser developer tools)
  • Delete your data by clearing your browser's local storage or signing out
  • Opt out of Google Sign-In at any time
  • Decline cookies through our cookie consent banner
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • The contact form on our website
  • Our social media channels (YouTube, GitHub, Twitter, Instagram)
